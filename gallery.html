ALFRED

Alfred is the butler of Wayne Manor, tends to the BatCave, and is the close confidant to the Dark Knight. After Bruce Wayne's parents died, Alfred became somewhat of a father figure to Batman. Alfred aids Batman on his missions through his varied skills

BATGIRL

Batgirl is the daughter of commissioner James Gordon, Barbara Gordon. After Batgirl got paralyzed by the Joker in a horrible accident, she became Oracle instead and assists Batman behind the scenes through her master hacking skills

BATMAN

After watching his parents get brutally murdered, Bruce Wayne resolved to fight crime in Gotham City as Batman. Known as the World's Greatest Detective, he is highly skilled and operates out of the Batcave, hidden under Wayne Manor

CATWOMAN

Originally known as Selina Kyle, Catwoman is a master burglar who frequently evades the Dark Knight. Her relationship with Batman is complicated--she is one of his biggest enemies while also being a pivotal love interest

HARLEY QUINN

Once a promising young psychiatrist, Dr. Harleen Quinzel met the Joker at Arkham Asylum. She fell madly in love with the super-villian and turned to a life of crime after helping him escape. Harley also maintains romantic relations with Poison Ivy

JOKER

After an unfortunate incident at Ace Chemical Plant, the Joker's appearance was completely transformed. As an insane comedian, he is one of the world's most dangerous psychopaths. Without Batman, crime wouldn't be nearly as fun

NIGHTWING

After Dick Grayson's parents were murdered in the circus, Batman adopted the young man. He trained to become Batman's sidekick, Robin, and eventually became a hero of his own right when he took up the mantle of Nightwing.

POISON IVY

After a childhood full of domestic abuse and violence, Pamela Isley turned to Botany in college. She became immune to toxins after a failed attempt to poison her. She harnesses the power of toxins and pheromones to manipulate anyone in her way.

TALIA AL GHUL

Daughter of Ra's Al Ghul, the leader of the League of Assassins, Talia Al Ghul is torn between her loyalty to her father and her infatuation with Batman. Talia frequently ossicilates between heroine and villainess in Batman's world.
